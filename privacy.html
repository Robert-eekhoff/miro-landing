Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Miiro ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

What We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and display name for authentication.
  • Household Data: Tasks, lists, recipes, meal plans stored in Firebase to sync across devices.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device token for delivering activity notifications.
  • Subscription Information: Processed by Apple. We receive status through RevenueCat, never your payment info.
  • Tell Miiro Input: When you use the Tell Miiro AI feature, your natural language input is sent to Anthropic's AI service for processing. This data is used solely to organize your tasks, meals, and grocery lists. Your input is not stored by Anthropic for training purposes and is processed in accordance with Anthropic's usage policy.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your location
  • Your browsing activity
  • Data from children under 13
  • We don't sell data to advertisers
  • We don't use data for targeted advertising
  • We don't store your Tell Miiro conversations after processing

How We Use Your Data

Solely to provide the Miiro app: syncing content, sharing with household members, notifications, and subscription management.

Data Storage

Stored securely on Google Firebase with encryption at rest and in transit. Access restricted to authenticated household members.

Data Sharing

We share data only with: Google Firebase (storage/auth), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Apple (payments), Anthropic (AI processing for Tell Miiro), and Zoho Mail (transactional emails). We never sell your data.

Your Rights

Request deletion anytime at support@miiro.app. Data deleted within 30 days. EU users have additional GDPR rights including access, rectification, and portability.

Changes

We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
