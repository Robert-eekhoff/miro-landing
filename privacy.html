Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Miro — Shared Tasks ("Miro", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Task and list data: The tasks, lists, recipes, and other content you create within the app are stored to provide our service.
  • Shared data: When you share lists with other users, we store the sharing relationship and the associated content.
  • Subscription data: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store your payment card details. We receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat, our subscription management provider.
  • Device information: We may collect basic device information for crash reporting and app performance improvement.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Miro app
  • Sync your tasks and lists across devices
  • Enable sharing features between you and the people you invite
  • Manage your subscription and provide access to paid features
  • Send important notifications about your account or shared tasks
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication). All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your data is restricted by security rules that ensure only you and your authorized shared list members can access your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With people you choose: When you share a list, the members you invite can see the content of that shared list.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for data storage and authentication, and RevenueCat for subscription management. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Third-Party Services

Miro uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and data storage — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Payment processing for subscriptions

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all the data stored in your account through the app.
  • Delete your data: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us or through the app settings.
  • Export your data: Request a copy of your data by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app at any time.

Children's Privacy

Miro is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of Miro after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
