What Miiro does

Miiro is a household management app for couples and families. Tell Sam, the built-in AI, what your household needs in plain language. "Pick up Miles at 3, milk on the list, dinner Thursday." Sam sorts it into tasks, calendar events, meal plans, and a grocery cart automatically. No app-switching, no shared notes, no nagging texts.

Miiro is built for the kind of mental load that doesn't fit a checklist. The constant tracking of what's in the fridge, who's picking up the kids, what's for dinner Thursday, when the package is being delivered. Most apps make you organize that load. Miiro takes it.

One subscription covers your whole household. Your partner joins with a link and gets full access. Grocery orders go to your store. Recipes come from TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. Calendar syncs with whatever you already use. All in your pocket, quiet and out of the way.
