The story

Why I built Miiro

Miiro comes from the Japanese word miru, to see.

I built it because my wife Mirrin and I kept losing track of the little things. The groceries, the household tasks, the recipes we wanted to try. Every task app out there was built for one person working alone. We needed something that let us see each other's day.

The name is also personal: Mi (from Mirrin) + Ro (from Robert) = Miiro. It's the app I wished existed, built for every person, couple, and family who's tired of things falling through the cracks.
